    • Kern On
      Tim Ahrens

      Kern On is a revolutionary kerning assistant that helps you cut down the time you spend kerning to just a few minutes. It takes care of your kern groups and your kerning. All it needs is a few kern pairs from you, then the plug-in ‘kerns on’ for you.

      Get Kern On →
      EUR 199
    • Remix Tools
      Tim Ahrens

      The Remix Tools are a set of three plugins: RMX Harmonizer, RMX Tuner, and RMX Scaler. While the Harmonizer irons out curve kinks, the Tuner and Scaler are indispensable tools for anyone working with Multiple Masters.

      Get the Remix Tools →
      EUR 99
    • LTTR/INK
      Martin Cetkovský and Filip Zajac

      Use stroke skeletons to speed up your work. LTTR/INK’s mighty nib force lets you draw with skeletons and control the stroke like never before. Forget outline editing, LTTR/INK generates perfectly plotted outlines, even directly to the exported font file.

      Get LTTR/INK →
      starting at EUR 50
